Frequently Asked Questions

What are the minimum requirements?

Minimum of 5 credits in WAEC/NECO including English and Mathematics, plus JAMB score of 180.

How long is the application process?

The entire process typically takes 6-8 weeks from application to admission decision.

Can I apply for multiple programs?

Yes, you can apply for up to two programs but will need to pay separate application fees.

Are there scholarship opportunities?

Yes, various merit-based and need-based scholarships are available for eligible students.
