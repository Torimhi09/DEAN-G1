Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow books?

Present your student ID at the circulation desk. Undergraduate students can borrow up to 5 books for 14 days.

Can I access e-resources off-campus?

Yes, use your university credentials to access all electronic resources remotely.

How do I book a study room?

Use our online booking system or visit the library information desk to reserve a study room.

What if I can't find a book?

Ask a librarian for help or request an inter-library loan if the book is not in our collection.
