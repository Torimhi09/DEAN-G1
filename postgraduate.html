Postgraduate Admissions

Welcome to Ilaro University's postgraduate studies. Our programs are designed to advance your academic and professional career through cutting-edge research and specialized knowledge.

Postgraduate Diploma (PGD)

Our PGD programs provide a bridge for professionals seeking to transition into new fields or enhance their qualifications.

Entry Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in any field (minimum of Second Class Lower)
  • Professional certification in relevant field
  • Two years work experience (recommended)

Doctoral Programs

Our Ph.D. programs focus on innovative research and academic excellence across various disciplines.

Entry Requirements

  • Master's degree in relevant field
  • Research proposal
  • Academic writing sample
  • Two academic references
