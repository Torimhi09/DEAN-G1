Biography

Professor Ibraheem Gbajabiamila, a distinguished academic and administrator, has served as Vice Chancellor of Ilaro University since 2022. With over 25 years of experience in higher education, he has led numerous initiatives to enhance academic excellence and research innovation.

Under his leadership, Ilaro University has achieved significant milestones, including:

  • Establishment of three new research centers
  • Implementation of digital transformation initiatives
  • Development of international academic partnerships
  • Enhancement of student support services

Key Achievements

  • Fellow, Nigerian Academy of Science
  • Recipient, National Merit Award for Educational Leadership
  • Author of over 100 peer-reviewed publications
  • Led successful accreditation of 15 new programs

Vision for Ilaro University
