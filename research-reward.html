Recognizing Excellence in Research

The Ilaro University Research Rewards Program celebrates outstanding contributions to academic research and innovation. Our program aims to support and encourage groundbreaking research across all disciplines.

Award Categories

Excellence in Research

Recognizing outstanding research contributions with significant impact in their field.

  • ₦5 million research grant
  • Publication support
  • Conference funding

Innovation Award

For innovative research projects with practical applications and societal impact.

  • ₦3 million grant
  • Patent support
  • Industry networking

Collaborative Research

Supporting cross-disciplinary research projects and international collaborations.

  • ₦4 million grant
  • Travel funding
  • Workshop support

Early Career Researcher

Supporting promising researchers in the early stages of their career.

  • ₦2 million grant
  • Mentorship program
  • Research training

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply?

All full-time faculty members and research staff at Ilaro University are eligible to apply.

When is the application deadline?

Applications for the 2024 program close on July 31, 2024.

How are winners selected?

Winners are selected through a rigorous peer-review process by our expert panel.

Can I apply for multiple categories?

Yes, researchers can apply for multiple categories but can only win one award per year.
