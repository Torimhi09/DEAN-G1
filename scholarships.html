Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for scholarships?

Both new and continuing students can apply, provided they meet the specific criteria for each scholarship type.

Can I apply for multiple scholarships?

Yes, you can apply for multiple scholarships, but you can only receive one at a time.

How is the scholarship renewed?

Scholarships are renewed annually based on maintaining the required academic performance and meeting other specified criteria.

What documents are required?

Required documents typically include academic transcripts, proof of income, recommendation letters, and a personal statement.
