Undergraduate Admissions

Welcome to Ilaro University's undergraduate admissions. We offer various pathways to join our prestigious institution and become part of our diverse academic community.

Regular Admission

Basic Requirements

  • Completed Secondary School Education
  • WAEC/NECO Results with Credits in Relevant Subjects
  • JAMB UTME Score of 180 and Above
  • Post-UTME Screening

Part-Time Programs

Our part-time programs offer flexible learning opportunities for working professionals and those unable to attend full-time classes.

Entry Requirements

  • O'Level Results
  • Relevant Work Experience
  • Basic Computer Literacy
